Skill Sets

PROTOTYPING

I enjoy putting together me and my teams ideas into a physical site for people to interact with through site like figma, outlining our ideas and showing the step by step process of how we got there.

STORYBOARD

It’s important to have an idea of what we want an audience to feel with a product, and storyboards are the best way to do that through analyzing user research and empathy data to create potential user scenarios.

WEB ANALYTICS

The best way to understand how a website works and were to improve is to see it for yourself. Interviewing users, going through user tests, and analyzing page layouts are key to iterating and adapting sites to a client’s needs.
